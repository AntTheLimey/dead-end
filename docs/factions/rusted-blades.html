Rusted Blades

other

Goals

    Members

    • Ihsan Soterios

    Overview

    A criminal gang that operates in the Thides System. They mostly scam wildcatter operations for protection money, threatening to wreck their operation, or inform on them to MacMillian. Most will just pay the “tax” but a few resist, till they are given the option to pay or get spaced. The gang operate below the radar of MacMillian security as they steer clear of interfering with the company, and it’s licensees.

    When not out running their protection, or hunting down new marks, they can be found at The Comet’s Tail.

    Kasey Garnett holds a particular grudge against the gang, as their antics threaten clients of hers, and she fears they might turn to MacMillian for protection. The fear is that they sell her out as well.
