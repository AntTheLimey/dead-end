Federal Republican News Corporation

Goals

    Overview

    A public/private corporation that operates under the FRBS as a nationwide clearing house for news. The FRNC is setup to broadcast 24/7 with offices on many worlds and stations. FRNC will often contract out on the ground reporting from local resources, then use their special relation with the FRBS to deliver the reports across the network.

    Due to the governmental nature of the organization, the FRNC content is managed by strict rules setup by the Federal Republic. While there is no formal censorship rules, there are unstated agreements the FRNC maintains with the government.
