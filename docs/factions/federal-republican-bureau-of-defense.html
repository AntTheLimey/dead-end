Federal Republican Bureau of Defense

Goals

    Overview

    The bureau responsible for the military of the Federal Republic. The republic maintains a large space fleet and a lesser number of marines for the defense and control of space. In most cases the republic allows for each world to maintain their own defense and security of the areas they control, but it requires the surrender of all the space between to the control of the republic.

    The republic for the most part focuses on the protection of travel lanes and commerce, preventing interruptions by criminal organizations as well as corporate disputes. The military will not actively investigate or enforce laws on those it encounters in space, it does accept the responsibility of mitigating any conflicts it comes across.

    In case where worlds enter into rebellion against the Federal Republic, the republic will use the military to enforce blockages, and in extreme cases invasions, of rebel worlds. These actions are not designed to crush or destroy these worlds, but to bring them back into line with the republic.
