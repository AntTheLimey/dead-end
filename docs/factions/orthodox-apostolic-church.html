Orthodox Apostolic Church

other

    Members

    • Surendra Antonescu

    Overview

    A faith founded on Thocronoe IV, one of the core foundational planets of the Federal Republic. The faith is a monotheistic religion based on the belief of one universal creator. This creator is responsible for the entire universe and all that resides in it. The faith teaches that the creator designed the universe to be self sustaining so no direct interference is needed. However, they are taught that through indirect means the creator will resolve issues they see.

    Due to their beliefs, members find a need to seek signs of the creator’s workings through the actions of others, and the actions of nature around them. Believers are supposed to understand their fallibility in interpreting these signs, but this is often disregarded by schismatic and heretical sects. Members are taught to that the importance is not in the reason of these signs, but in seeing them and understanding the creators ongoing plan. These actions represent that the creator continues to perfect his creation, and that no future is determined because of these changes.
