The Far Dark

1

Four down-on-their-luck strangers take the only job left to them — “security” for MacMillian Mining’s last rusting station at the frayed dead-end of charted space, in the Thides System. It looks like a chance to rebuild a life on a rotten little boomtown that, against the odds, is easy to love. The dead end has other plans.

This is the player-facing landing for Chapter 1. The chapter’s story unfolds session by session in the recaps below.
