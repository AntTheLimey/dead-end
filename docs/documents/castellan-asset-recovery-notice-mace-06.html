Castellan - Asset Recovery Notice (MACE-06) Draft

An in-world prop from Six’s background. Castellan Biodynamics, Asset Protection Division. The reclamation claim on unit MACE-06.

The document (in-world)

CASTELLAN BIODYNAMICS  ///  MERIDIAN WORKS
ASSET PROTECTION DIVISION
──────────────────────────────────────────────
NOTICE OF UNRECOVERED PROPERTY — CONFIDENTIAL
Ref: AP/06-MACE/RECOV        Priority: STANDING

CLASS ........ Boarding Asset, MACE-pattern
UNIT ......... vat-batch 06  (designation "MACE-06")
STATUS ....... UNRECOVERED. Removed from secured
               cryostorage during unauthorised entry,
               Meridian Works Vault 9. Not scrapped.
               Not decommissioned. Company property.
VALUE ........ Recoverable. A recovery fee is payable
               on verified return to inventory. Terms
               on application to the Division.

IDENTIFYING MARKS
  Etched maker's mark and batch code, subdermal:
  inner right forearm and nape beneath the hairline.
  Reads: CASTELLAN // MACE-06.
  A deep bioscan or records pull confirms non-human
  manufacture. The unit passes visual inspection as
  a human male, approx. 190 cm, densely built.

HANDLING — READ BEFORE APPROACH
  The unit carries a full combat conditioning load.
  It is not defective and it is not docile. Do NOT
  attempt physical recovery with untrained personnel.
  Report a sighting. Do not engage. Recovery is a
  specialist contract.

  The unit was conditioned to believe its pattern is
  preserved after end of service. Maintaining that
  belief improves compliance. Do not discuss the
  matter with the asset.

CONTACT
  Any Castellan-registered broker, quoting the Ref
  above. Discretion is a condition of the fee.
──────────────────────────────────────────────
This notice is the property of Castellan Biodynamics.
Possession by an unlicensed party is itself an offence.
