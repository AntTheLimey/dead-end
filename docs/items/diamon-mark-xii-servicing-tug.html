Diamon Mark XII Servicing Tug

vehicle

Overview

A port tug used to in orbit or around stations to handle ship traffic. The tugs are built with a re-enforced forward electromagnetic hull that allows the ship to connect and push ships in port. In addition that contain a grappling arm and multiple drone docks. These ships are used to move freight and ships around the port, assist with ships navigation in port, and to conduct repairs.

Criminal elements often modify these ships for use boarding other ships.
