Solveig Kaufer

Overview

Assistant to the Station Manager of MacMillian IV, she is the main person running the station as her boss Wilton offloaded his work on her to add to her work total. Because of this overt trust by Wilton, Solveig is given a free hand running the station. While she might be the ultimate power of the station, she has gone out of her way to run the station efficiently and fairly. All actions appear reasoned and justified, and bringing no rebuke from corporate or the feds.

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — Solveig Kaufer introduced herself to the crew in Conference Room A as General Secretary and assistant to Wilton Agramunt, then ran their orientation and station tour with precise, by-the-book thoroughness, issuing each a MacMillian company card and noting security was at roughly half strength (52 of 106). As she left, she was overheard phoning Holger Graeme to report protocol violations — the list of infractions Six had handed her on his way in. The crew later saw her at The Silk Abbey, visibly frustrated, chasing Agramunt for signatures he gave without looking.
