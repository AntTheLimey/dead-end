Overview

Current manager of MacMilian Station IV, and director in the Macmillian company. He sees his job as a dead end, and just hopes the company survives long enough for him to retire. Oblivious to the goings on around the station, he leans more on his directory secretary to run the day to day operations. Little or none of the station business reach him, leaving him not only obtuse, but also mis-informed about what is going on around him.

He always dresses impeccably, as a company director of a larger more successful corporation would, far outside someone of his current status would.

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — The crew first met Wilton Agramunt only as a face in a dated, looping orientation video welcoming new arrivals to MacMillian Station IV; in person he was absent, said to be occupied with “personal business.” Later that evening they spotted him at The Silk Abbey’s high-roller level, a woman on either side, distractedly signing station documents that Solveig Kaufer kept pushing at him and waving her off between sips of his drink. He came across as thoroughly disengaged from the running of the station.
