Overview

Primary commander of the group named after her, the Akker Cartel. She is a by the book operator with a long experienced history in first military operations, and then later criminal ones. Her planning is meticulous, and all aspects of an operation are reviewed before taking any job.

Motivations & Secrets

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — Daniela Akkermans passed through Security Gate 1 during the crew’s first shift, screened alongside three companions, all four carrying honorable-discharge papers from the Federal Marines. Her service record scanned as long — decades of it — but almost entirely redacted, saying nothing beyond the fact that she had served. Later that night the crew noticed her again at the Nova Nexus, deep in a private conversation with unknown individuals in a corner.
