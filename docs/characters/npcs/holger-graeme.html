Overview

Chief of station security, he’s trying his best to keep the station and system safe and ordered as possible, even if it’s a hopeless task.

The crew met him as a blunt, pragmatic operator who ran the “real” briefing over corporate formality — he told them to forget Solveig Kaufer’s orientation entirely and gave them the rules that actually keep security alive on MacMillian Station IV. He wears his patience thin easily, and Six’s granular protocol questions visibly frayed it.

Motivations & Secrets

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — Holger Graeme delivered the crew’s real briefing in Conference Room A, opening by telling them to forget everything Solveig Kaufer had said. His rules were simple: don’t escalate, don’t go into a fight you can’t finish, call for backup, and don’t make problems where there aren’t any. He named the station’s key figures and threats — Samina Ventura of The Silk Abbey as a high-priority client, Asher Bhattacharya and Plen Hong around the Nova Nexus / Serene Syndicate, the Rusted Blades in the belt, and scavenger Thandeka Dahl near Thides I — before Six’s relentless follow-up questions frayed his patience and he dismissed the team.
