Overview

The reigning bare-knuckle boxing champion of the Thides system — a veteran fighter with clearly superior technique, whose bouts are broadcast into the Nova Nexus holographic viewing pit. The crew watched him win what read plainly as a fixed matchup against a young newcomer.

Motivations & Secrets

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — At the Nova Nexus, Six and Rock Lavey watched a holo-broadcast bout in which Clubber, the veteran champion, faced a young newcomer. Both read the matchup the same way — a setup engineered to hand the veteran an easy win before a much bigger match in the coming weeks. Clubber put the newcomer down in the third round, exactly as they had called it.
