Overview

One time wildcatter, Roland found more success in running The Comet’s Tail than any mining claim he ever worked. No one knows how he came to own The Comet’s Tail, but they do know it’s officially his. His laissez-faire attitude towards patron’s actions makes his establishment one of the more lively on the station. He has only two rules, don’t annoy him and don’t bring the cops. Anyone breaking those rules will soon find themselves on the other end of the pickaxe he keeps behind the bar.

Roland once served as a marine in the Federal Military where he lost his arms. He’s never been clear as to how he lost them, the story changes every time he tells it.
