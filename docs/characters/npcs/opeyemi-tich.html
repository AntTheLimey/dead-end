Overview

Criminal boss, and owner of the Nova Nexus, Opeyemi has their hand in many of the more illegal operations in the system. He’s most suspected of arranging the shipments of Wildcatter production out of the system without having paid the company fees, netting himself and his clients a nice additional profit. In addition to his “business” ventures, he holds influence in the Serene Syndicate, an inter-steller criminal organization.
