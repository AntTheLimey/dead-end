Ihsan Soterios

Role Member of Rusted Blades Status alive
Second in command and muscle of the Rusted Blades.

Overview

Second in command and muscle of the Rusted Blades. Ihsan grew up in the slums of Processor I. After meeting Leocadia he found more opportunities in her company. He is fiercely loyal to Leocadia, as she pulled him out of poverty. While he appears to play the part of dumb brute, there is a hidden cunning in his actions
