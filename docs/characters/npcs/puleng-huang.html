Overview

An underling of Opeyemi in the Thides System, the trickier of the two. She joined the Syndicate as a teenager in the Bellerophon System. After too many run ins with the local government law enforcement due to her hot temper, she had to flee to avoid arrest and imprisonment. Escaping into the Federal Republic, she bounced around between syndicate groups until landing in the Thides system. Here Opeyemi found her after an introduction by on of his “friends”. Opeyemi found her penchant for violence to be useful. While not as strong and physically imposing as many members of the Syndicate, the ability to be crafty makes her more deadly.
