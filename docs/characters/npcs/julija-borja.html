Overview

Ambitious Marshal of the Federal Republic, she is a by the book official and finds it difficult operating in a company controlled system. Her lack of direct authority outside of limited areas forces her to spend most time doing paperwork reporting actions of the company, as well as arranging the deportations from the Thides System. She is effectively the only representative of the Federal Republic in the system.
