Overview

Docking chief of MacMillian Station IV, she has worked for the company all her life, and wants to look out for her “boys” on the dock. Nothing gets on/off the station without her signoff, or at least a confirmation. Her concern for the future of the company, and the job at the station leaves her worried.

Five years ago she lead a strike of the dock workers that got them better pay. Since then the company has atrophied her force by not replacing retires, fired, or quitting workers. This would lead to her often behind schedule in getting the shipments out, if the number of ships didn’t decrease over the years. It has, however, made her less able to be as thorough as she wants, something the more opportunistic have taken advantage of for both legal and illegal means.
