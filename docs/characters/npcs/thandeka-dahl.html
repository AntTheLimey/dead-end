Thandeka Dahl

Role Owner of The Beloved Valley Status alive
The operator of the salvage ship "The Beloved Valley" in the Thides System.

Overview

The operator of the salvage ship “The Beloved Valley” in the Thides System. Like a bunch of operators, she has moved to the system to hope to profit off the collapse of the MacMillian company. She spends most of her time looking for old abandoned mining operations to salvage.
