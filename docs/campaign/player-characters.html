Player Characters Draft

PCs not yet created. Build in Session 0. This roster fills in as players make characters.

Build Parameters (GURPS 4e, Space)

  • Points: 175 / up to 75 in disadvantages.
  • TL11, limited super-science (mostly FTL). Biotech, cybernetics, nanotech all permitted.
  • No Wealth, no Status, no Rank, no Independent Income. Start with the clothes on your back and a backpack — the down-on-their-luck frame.
  • Citizens by default. Take Second-Class Citizen to play a non-citizen (resident) — meaningful in this setting (no federal protection; deportable).
  • The hook: each PC is desperate enough to take a “security” job at a dying frontier station — struck out in business, can’t hold a job, on the run, or worse. Define why.
  • The crew: a four-person security team hired together (arrived on the same shuttle). Suggested spread — the talker, the bruiser, the tech, the sharp eye — but flex to taste.
