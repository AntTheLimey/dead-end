Chapter 1 — The Far Dark

  • (in-game date TBD) — Session 1 — Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space — The crew (Six, Rock Lavey, Shackleton Magellan, Ronin Sanchez) arrive at MacMillian Station IV by separate routes and take the MacMillian Security job. Orientation under Solveig Kaufer, the real briefing from Chief Holger Graeme, a first gate shift, and a night through the Entertainment District (The Comets Tail, The Silk Abbey, Nova Nexus). Over the following weeks the crew maps the station’s selective-enforcement corruption via the Law Machine and clocks the local players: the Rusted Blades under Leocadia Zavia, the Serene Syndicate around the Nova Nexus, and the redacted-record Marine Daniela Akkermans.
