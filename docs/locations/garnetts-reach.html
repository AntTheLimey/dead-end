Overview

A “secret” outpost in the Thidian Belt that operates clandestine operations under the company’s nose. While formally under Kasey Garnett’s rule, the place is more of a collection of independent operators running their own enterprises. MacMillian knows of it’s existence in the system, but is unable to determine it’s location.

People generally don’t visit the station. Instead they will contact and meetup somewhere in the system with representatives of those that have access to the outpost. These representatives are authorized to complete deals, and arrange the exchange of goods and currency. It is rare that items from these deals are brought back to the outpost, more often they are shipped on to the next person in the chain. Those operating out of the outpost more often act as middle men in the transactions, than actual direct dealers.
