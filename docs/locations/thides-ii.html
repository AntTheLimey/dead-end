Overview

A world wrapped in a toxic, corrosive atmosphere — brutal, equipment-eating, and lethal to the careless. The work here chews through gear and people; labor is expendable and “accidents” are cheap.

Points of Interest

Sealed surface extraction sites; graveyards of corroded equipment; pressurized work-shacks where crews shelter between shifts.

A Terrestrial world with a thick corrosive atmosphere, the planet itself has not been mined, even though it is open to access. This is mostly due to the cost of maintenance on equipment and the lack of quality surveys of surface. Most current prospecting returned less than optimal results, even if an occasional wildcatter will make an attempt. It is considered a level 3, dangerous but safe for access with the correct equipment
