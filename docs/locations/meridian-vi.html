Overview

The far, hostile outer world of the Meridian system — an extreme-temperature rock with a tidally-locked moon. A remote Castellan Biodynamics black-site: hazard extraction, and where written-off assets and inconvenient things go to be forgotten. The Charter Gate — the system’s quiet corporate gate — lies out in the dark beyond its orbit.
