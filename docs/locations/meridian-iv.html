Overview

The capital of the Meridian system — a large rocky world, habitable… for now. Seat of the Meridian Concord, Castellan Biodynamics HQ, the arcology enclaves of the shareholding families, and the asset districts that run it all. Two greenbelt preserves straddle the 40° line — manicured green standing in for the lush flora the world used to have (or wants to be remembered for); Castellan’s Greenmarch foundries (where Six was grown) sit against the northern belt. And the world’s orbit is slowly decaying — a doom no one with a charter seat cares to discuss.
