The Silk Abbey

Overview

On of the most popular drinking establishment on MacMillian Station IV, its a cover for the brothel that earns the madame Samina Ventura the most of her income. Each “private” room is named to try to bring it some high class status, but this place would be a low quality establishment if not for the other company it keeps. Inside it plays warmer and more upscale than its exterior suggests — a Middle-Eastern aesthetic, with a high-roller upper level where the real money moves.

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — The crew’s second stop on their night out. Madam Samina Ventura’s house comped each of them a complimentary service — the same kickback culture running through the whole Entertainment District. On the upper level, Station Manager Wilton Agramunt sat with two women, signing documents Solveig Kaufer pressed on him without reading them. Rock Lavey clocked patrons discreetly using illicit substances — mostly libido enhancers, a few closer to a dangerous amphetamine — with no notice from staff or security.
