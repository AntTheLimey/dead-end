MacMillian Station Security Office

Station Facility
Overview The headquarters of MacMillian station security aboard MacMillian Station IV and the crew’s workplace.

Overview

The headquarters of MacMillian station security aboard MacMillian Station IV and the crew’s workplace. It holds an intake and statement-processing area, three holding cells sealed behind red laser bars, the private office of Security Chief Holger Graeme, and Conference Room A — where orientation and the daily briefings run. A front-desk clerk screens walk-ins. The Marshal’s Office sits directly across the corridor.

Points of Interest

  • Conference Room A — orientation and morning briefings
  • Three holding cells behind red laser bars
  • Chief Holger Graeme’s office
  • Intake / statement-processing area
  • The automated booking terminal (the station “law machine”)

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — The crew reported here for induction: Solveig Kaufer’s formal orientation in Conference Room A, then Chief Holger Graeme’s blunter next-morning briefing that told them to forget most of it. Six arrived early, talking his way past the front-desk clerk after slipping in through the station’s tampered stairwells. Working the booking terminal later, the crew found it dismissed charges against connected individuals while penalizing independent miners for identical offenses.
