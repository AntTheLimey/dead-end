The Comets Tail

venue

Overview

One time the best establishment on the station, its now home to some of the roughest individuals in the system. Operated by Roland Mohamed, it’s place you’ll leave with a hangover, a bloody nose, and no money. Repurposed industrial mining bots run food and drinks to the tables, and the kitchen still turns out surprisingly good fare — lobster, steak, and pasta.

Points of Interest

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — The crew’s first stop on their night out. Bartender Roland Mohamed — two cybernetic arms — recognized them as the new security detail, comped their drinks and food, and shared intel on local smuggling and the Rusted Blades. At a corner table, Leocadia Zavia of the Rusted Blades held court, quietly extracting coordinates from a captive miner before her crew marched him out; Roland confirmed she runs the Blades out of Processing I.
