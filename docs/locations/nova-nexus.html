Nova Nexus

venue
Overview A gambling establishment and nightclub located on MacMillian Station IV.

Overview

A gambling establishment and nightclub located on MacMillian Station IV. It is owned by the biggest known criminal boss in the sector, Opeyemi Tichá. While intended to be a front operation, it’s surprise popularity on the station makes it as valueable as any other of Opeyemi’s enterprises.While considered upscale, its just as dirty as any other establishment on the station, this just has music, neon, and a dance floor. The floor the crew walked was fronted by a DJ, a holographic sports-betting pit, and table games — roulette, craps, and poker. Chief Holger Graeme flagged it to the crew as suspected of ties to the Serene Syndicate.

Appearances

In the holographic boxing pit a bout looked fixed: the veteran Clubber was set up to put down a young newcomer ahead of a bigger match, and he did, in the third round.
