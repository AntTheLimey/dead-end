A habitable terrestrial world that possesses a breathable atmosphere, Thides III is unoccupied by being declared a restricted planet, due to dangerous flora and fauna. Many of the creatures, and some of the plant, have a natural affinity for electromagnetic waves. These seem to draw the flora and fauna toward sources of the waves. This draw, along with the deadly nature of these creatures, makes establishment of any human activity and difficult, leading Federal scientists to place restrictions on the plant. It is considered a level 1 deadly restrictions due to the flora and fauna.
