Overview

An asteroid belt and one of the two currently actively mined areas of the system. The belt consists of a number of large and small objects that many wildcatters and small subcontractors extract minerals from. Most are low deposit sources, making it unproductive for larger operations. It is located between Thiden III and Thiden IV.

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — Named and briefed by Chief Holger Graeme (as the “Thides Belt”) as the system’s primary claim-dispute patrol zone. He flagged a hidden smuggler base suspected somewhere in the belt, with connections back on the station — nobody has found it yet. Not visited this session.
