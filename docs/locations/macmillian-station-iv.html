MacMillian Station IV

Space Station
Overview The only remaining station owned by the MacMillian Mining and Extraction Corporation from their once mighty fleet of operations.

Overview

The only remaining station owned by the MacMillian Mining and Extraction Corporation from their once mighty fleet of operations. It’s located in the Thides system, and continues to operate along with their operations in the system. The station has seen better times, but it still general operational. The station contains:

Landing hangers for smaller ships Docking hookups for large ships Services for ships (repair and fueling) Entertainment district Processing/Shipping factory for mining operations in the system Re-supply options for subcontractor and wildcatters working in this system Residences for station staff and visitors Security offices for the company Fed marshal office

As the crew observed on arrival, the station is laid out in four levels — the public entertainment and hotel district up top, station personnel below it, the cargo and warehouse level beneath that, and the processing units at the bottom, of which only units 3 and 4 still run. Security was running at roughly half strength: 52 personnel against a required 106.

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — The crew — Rock Lavey, Shackleton Magellan, Six, and the late-arriving Ronin Sanchez — reported here to take up MacMillian security posts. Rock and Shack thawed from cryostasis in the Docking Ring passenger terminal; Six smuggled himself in as cargo on the corporate cargo level. Induction ran through Solveig Kaufer’s formal orientation and Chief Holger Graeme’s blunter next-morning briefing, and the crew pulled their first shift screening arrivals at Security Gate 1.

Places Within

Entertainment District

The top-ring public strip of MacMillian Station IV — neon and noise crammed into a single corridor of gambling dens, bars, and service establishments, smelling of spilled drink and opportunity.

MacMillian Station Security Office

The headquarters of MacMillian station security aboard MacMillian Station IV and the crew's workplace.

Marshal's Office

The Federal Republic of Worlds' official presence aboard MacMillian Station IV, directly across the corridor from the MacMillian Station Security Office.

Church of Saint Barbara

A chapel located on the MacMillian Station IV, it is a small space built out of a former residence.

Docking Ring

The outermost ring of the station, the docking ring is where most of the ships arrive at the station.

Processing I

First of the four processing plants located in the lower section of the station.

Stamp Supply

Owned and operated by Khasan Stamp, it is the major source of supplies for those conducting operations in the system.

The Main Line

The one time main path of the processing facility, it has become a sort of main street to the slums of MacMillian Station IV.
