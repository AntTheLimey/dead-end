Overview

A ringed gas giant with a clutch of actively-mined moons — the system’s living present and the busiest neighborhood in Thides. This is where the work, the traffic, and Thides Station all actually are.

Points of Interest

The mined moons; Thides Station (MacMillian’s hub); skimming and extraction platforms.

Appearances

mobRPG (canon import)

A gas giant with multiple moons. The strength of the gravity around the planet, and it’s strong wind, makes is impractical to harvest gas from. The remaining large mining operations by the company in the system exist among it’s moons.

  • Contains Thides Station — The system's hub sits in this neighborhood.
