Overview

The top-ring public strip of MacMillian Station IV — neon and noise crammed into a single corridor of gambling dens, bars, and service establishments, smelling of spilled drink and opportunity. Security patrols the edges but watches with practiced disinterest, tolerating petty crime — pickpockets, shell games, panhandlers slipping guards a credit chip — so long as nothing boils over. A pervasive kickback culture runs through the whole strip: comped drinks, meals, chips, and services flow to security personnel in exchange for a blind eye. It houses Nova Nexus, The Comets Tail, and The Silk Abbey.

Points of Interest

  • Nova Nexus — the station’s largest gambling house
  • The Comets Tail — the faded former-finest bar
  • The Silk Abbey — the upscale high-roller establishment

Appearances

  • Session 1 (Session 01 - The Ragged Edge of Space) — Rock Lavey and Shackleton Magellan were escorted through the district on arrival, past the Marshal’s Office and into induction. Later, off shift, the crew worked the strip in turn — The Comets Tail, The Silk Abbey, then Nova Nexus — and clocked the kickback economy running through every venue.
