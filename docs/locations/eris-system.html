Eris I

A former gas giant that had its thick atmosphere completely stripped away by intense stellar winds.

Eris II

Locked in a runaway greenhouse effect, this world features crushing atmospheric pressure and a thick yellow fog of vaporized sulfur dioxide.

Eris III

The habitable zone world of the system.

Eris IV

A cold, dry planet covered in fine iron oxide dust, featuring massive canyons and extinct shield volcanoes.

Eris V

A massive gas giant composed almost entirely of hydrogen and helium, featuring bands of striking copper, orange, and cream clouds.

Eris VI

A massive outer planet primarily composed of ammonia, methane, and water ice.

Eris VII

Positioned at the very edge of the Orange Dwarf's light, this world is a freezing wasteland of nitrogen ice, methane snow, and jagged obsidian-like rock formations.

Erisian Belt

A massive planetary ring system created billions of years ago when two large ice moons collided.
