Sargasso Void

gravitational anomaly

Overview

The Sargasso Void is the massive, uncharted gravitational anomaly the Thides System orbits — a dark, enormous nothing that swallows light and defies navigation, sitting at the ragged edge of explored space in Sector 7-G. It is the reason Thides is a charted dead end: no one has successfully mapped a way through it.

What is publicly known is thin. Gravity behaves strangely inside it. It reads as a huge mass with nothing visible to account for it, which the common assumption chalks up to dark matter. There is standing speculation that the void conceals planets no one can see. Beyond that — a name, weird gravity, and unseen worlds in theory — nothing about the Sargasso is established.
