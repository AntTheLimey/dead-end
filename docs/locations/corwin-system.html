Corwin System

star system
A Binary start system with no habitable planets, the system possesses three gates

Overview

A Binary start system with no habitable planets, the system possesses three gates

Points of Interest

Places Within

Corwin A

A bright yellow-white F-type star (1.3 solar masses) providing the bulk of the system's heat and light.

Corwin B

A cooler, orange K-type star (0.7 solar masses).

Corwin Gate II

A gate that leads to the Corwin-Eris route.

Corwin Gate III

The third gate in the Corwin system, this gate leads to the Corwin-Thides Route

Corwin I

A tidally locked, airless rocky world heavily cratered by meteorites.

Corwin II

The second and larger rocky world.

Corwin III

A large gas giant with fifteen moons, as well as an impressive set of rings.

Corwin IV

An giant frozen gas giant

Corwin V

A dwarf planet one the outer edge of the system, it has an irregular orbit in that it's more oval with an extreem difference between it's narrowest and furthest distance to the center of the system.
